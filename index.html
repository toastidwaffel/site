What You Need

  • A few Meta Quest 2, 3, or 3S headsets with copies of the game.
  • Recommended 14m x 24m (79' x 46') space or larger. (10m x 10m minimum)
  • Consistent Wi-Fi coverage throughout your space. (Internet access recommended, but not required.)
  • (Recommended) mid-range gaming laptop with Steam edition of the game as a dedicated server and spectator feed. (But a headset can host if needed).
