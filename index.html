About The Game

Catastrophic Inversion is a multiplayer VR arena shooter you can bring to a gym or other large space and really walk around in: no joysticks or teleporting.


It comes with more than 10 virtual arenas with moving parts like elevators to take you to second-story platforms or gondolas to carry you across chasms, all while allowing multiple players to move around within the same playspace. (We've tested up to 10 players but there's no hard limit.)


Alignment is handled entirely within the Quest headset and requires no external sensors or equipment (other than adequate Wi-Fi coverage for a local network connection between devices).

Get The Game



Recommended Setup

  • Meta Quest 2, 3, or 3S headsets with the Quest edition of the game for each player.
  • A 14m x 24m (79' x 46') playspace or larger. (There are also some simpler 10m x 10m maps and a traditional standing VR mode if you don't have that much space.)
  • Consistent Wi-Fi coverage throughout your space. (Internet access recommended, but not required.)
  • A mid-range gaming laptop with the Steam edition of the game as a dedicated server and spectator feed. (A headset could host if needed, but it's less reliable.')
